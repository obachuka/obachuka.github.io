Screen resolutions

by Obachuka

Something I've always wondered was how 2D games handled screen resolutions. For 3D games, it doesn't matter as much since polygons can be scaled up, but images can't. I've seen Transistor master their art at both 720p and 1080p. Requiring HD at minimum seems common for modern games, which is reasonable. I doubt gamers will be using tiny screens, and even if they did, they should be well aware that some games just don't work on small screens.

But what about aspect ratio? For some games, elements can be rearranged, but for games like side-scrolling shooters, changing the aspect ration would affect the gameplay since the screen acts as the boundary for the playing field. This is a problem I'm having with my current game. I use vertical bars, but can't decide what to put in them. Having black bars is just ugly.
