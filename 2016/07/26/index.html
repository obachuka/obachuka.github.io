Invincible spikes

by Obachuka

I introduced invincible spikes today. Spikes means a dot is invincible, while a gray outline means that a dot can turn invincible.

The player dot uses the same spikes and outline to denote when the player is attacking and when the player is ready to attack.

I made invincible dots to introduce a different type of difficulty in later levels. That's right, my game will have levels. Levels is my last "necessary" feature before I am willing to say my game is in a finished state.
